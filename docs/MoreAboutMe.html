Outside of graphic design who am I?

I am a student athlete that deals with mental health issues that I try to advocate for and spread awareness to.

How do I spread awareness towards mental health in athletes?

  • I have a suicide awareness ribbon on the back of my helmet
  • I let people know it's okay not to be okay
  • I voice when I am struggling so others don't feel alone if they are secretly fighting their own battles
  • I make viral tiktok videos about student-athletes mental health and use my platform to grow awareness
  • When I see people struggling I give them the same techniques I use and just be there for them.
  • My glove is suicide awareness colors

Below is five reasons why I spread awareness to this issue.

  1. Around 50% of athletes aree reported to struggle with mental health
  2. In a 20 year span, 128 college athletes commited suicide
  3. In two months alone of 2022, five college athletes died by suicide
  4. There is a bag stigma around athletes regarding mental health because they are viewed as weak if they ask for help
  5. Around 1/3 of college athletes reported having high anxiety
