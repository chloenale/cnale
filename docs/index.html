What are some things student-athletes deal with?

There is a negative stigma student-atheltes face when it comes to their mental health. Below is a list of things student-athelets may face.

  • depresion
  • anxiety
  • exhaustion
  • burn outs
  • low self-esteem

Below is a list of ways I deal with these stressors since I'm more likely to experience them as a student-athlete.

  1. Building my mental toughness
  2. Talking to a therapist specializing in athletes
  3. Following a routine and scuedule
  4. Focus on my breathing
  5. Practicing time-management
  6. Getting enough sleep
